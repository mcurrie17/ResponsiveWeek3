...a little about me

I'm a project manager and mom of two living in Austin, TX. I relocated in 2018 from the Northeast to avoid the cold winters and enjoy a well rounded lifestyle for young kids.

Married with two toddlers, I'm a survivor of the 2 under 2 club! The girls keep me on my toes and have been absolute troopers during the many hours I've put in to complete my certifications.

Now focused on expanding my career options, I've brushed off my very basic dev skills learned in my Fine Arts college program and used SheCodes to expand them further. I've successfully completed Basics, Plus, and am nearly finished with Responsive. Next up I will learn React and hope to begin freelancing to grow my skills and build a strong portfolio.

My ultimate goal is to break into Front End Development full time and find meaningful work on beautiful, user friendly applications.
